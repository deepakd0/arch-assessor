Scene 1 — The idea takes shape done 2026-07-02

A platform that assesses architectures against an organisation's own guardrails and turns findings into compliance evidence. Customer, wedge, and constraints locked in.

  • ✓Target customer chosen: mid-size tech companies (200–2000 people, no formal EA team)
  • ✓MVP wedge: assessment & validation engine; scanners ingested via SARIF, never rebuilt
  • ✓Framework order set: SOC 2 → ISO 27001 → PCI-DSS → HIPAA/GDPR
  • ○Phase 0 validation: 10–15 customer interviews to confirm the wedge before further buildPRD §6
